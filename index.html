todocmd

A Todo App That Learns Your Habits

View project onGitHub

What Is It?

Todocmd is an intelligent todo app that organizes your todos based on your routines. It is designed to increase productivity and simplify your workflow.

How Does It Work?

When ever you complete a todo, information about it is saved. The priority, keywords, and other aspects from each todo are used by the sorting algorithm to determine how to organize your todos.

What Is Next?

Future versions of todocmd will improve todo sorting and take aspects like time completed into consideration when organizing your todos. Additional improvements, such as a note functionality, are also planned.
