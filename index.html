What It Does

Todocmd takes your schedule into its own hands, leaving you with a little extra time. Once you start using tococmd, it will start to learn your routines and optimize your todos/schedule. Todos are automatically sorted and organized for you, although you always have the ability to override this. With any luck, you will become more productive and better organized with todocmd.
